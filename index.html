Welcome!

Welcome to the first puzzle of this escape room! To proceed to the other puzzles you must first complete mine!

At the start of the game, you will be given a 6 digit 'guide number'.

This number will be your tool to solve the puzzle.

The puzzle consists of three sets of random letter sequences.

It is your job to work out which numbers from the 'guide number', when added to each of your scrambled letters, move forward through the alphabetic sequence to make up a 6 letter number in word format.

For example FOURTY

The aim of the puzzle is to find three of these 6 letter numbers.

HINT: A+3=D

Use this technique to try adding different numbers from the 'guide number' to each of the letters in the scrambled sequences.

HINT: The alphabet wheel can be used to count.

Once you have entered your 6 letter number in the answer box, press enter. The box will go green if you are correct, red if you are not! You have 8 minutes to complete this!
